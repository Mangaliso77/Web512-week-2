Quarterly Theory in Trading

The market moves in **quarterly cycles**. Each 3-month period gives a macro bias. If you learn to align your trades with the quarterly shift, you’ll trade with the market instead of against it.

  • 📅 Identify quarterly highs and lows.
  • 🕒 Focus only inside your killzone (time of day that matters).
  • 📉 Trade with the quarterly bias – not against it.
